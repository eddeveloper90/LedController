1 # LedController {#mainpage}
2 
3 LedController is an [Arduino](http://arduino.cc) library for MAX7219 and MAX7221 Led display drivers.
4 The code should work with most boards and architectures but I only test it for the ESP32 Dev Module.
5 Feel free to create an issue if something is missing or not working correctly.
6 
7 The CI automatically tests the following boards(PlatformIO boards names):
8 
9 * esp32dev
10 * d1_mini
11 * uno
12 * ATmega1280
13 * leonardo
14 
15 The following boards do **not** work at the moment:
16 
17 * due
18 
19 All help regarding compatibility with more boards is welcome.

29 ## Hardware SPI Pin Assignments
30 
31 If you want to use hardware SPI you have to use the following port.
32 Please keep in mind that the MOSI(Master out Slave in) and the SCK(Serial Clock) signal can be shared by multiple SPI devices, while the SS(Slave Select) signal must be unique to every device(You can use every free pin for this).
33 
34 | Arduino Board | MOSI | MISO | SCK | SS (Slave) | SS (Master) |
35 |---------------|------|------|-----|------------|-------------|
36 | Uno or Duemilanove | 11 or ICSP-4 | 12 or ICSP-1 | 13 or ICSP-3 | 10 | - |
37 | Mega1280 or Mega2560 | 51 or ICSP-4 | 50 or ICSP-1 | 52 or ICSP-3 | 53 | - |
38 | Leonardo | ICSP-4 | ICSP-1 | ICSP-3 | - | - |
39 | Due | ICSP-4 | ICSP-1 | ICSP-3 | - | 4, 10, 52 |
40 | Deek-Robot Arduino | 11 | 12 | 13 | - | 10 |
41 | ESP32-WROOM | 23 | 19 | 18 | - | 5 |
