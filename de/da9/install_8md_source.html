5 ## Arduino Ide
6 
7 The library is in the Arduino library manager.
8 
9 1. Open your [Arduino](http://arduino.cc) IDE.
10 2. Click on Tools, Manage Libraries..
11 3. Type `LedController` in the search bar.
12 4. Select the version you want and click install.
13 5. That was it you are ready to use the library.

15 ## PlatformIO
16 
17 The [library](https://platformio.org/lib/show/7144/LedController) is also available for the [platform.io](https://platformio.org/platformio) library manager.
18 **Since they get their information from the Arduino Library Manager and the git repository, their version might not be up to date.**

21
